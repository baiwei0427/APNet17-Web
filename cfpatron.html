1st Asia-Pacific Workshop on Networking (APNet 2017)

August 3-4 2017, Hong Kong, China

Call for Patrons

APNet offers different support package possibilities that can suit organizations of any size. These packages offer an opportunity for the supporting organization, to promote its visibility, services and products to a large community. It is also an opportunity to demonstrate support to research and development activities in the computer networking community.

Sponsorship Levels

  • Platinum: USD$10,000
  • Gold: USD$6,000
  • Silver: USD$3,000
  • In-kind Sponsorships (support of a particular activity at the conference)

Sponsorship Benefits

All sponsors will be listed in the Conference Program, ACM Conference Proceedings and on the Conference Web site, with a link to the sponsor's company Home Page. Depending on the level of sponsorship, a sponsor will receive the following additional benefits:

Platinum Sponsor

  • Recognized as Platinum Supporter at the conference banquet
  • Large poster indicating Platinum Sponsorship at registration booth
  • Three complimentary conference registrations for the conference
  • Display space during the conference
  • Any number of company-provided items in conference bag

Gold Sponsor

  • Recognized as Gold Supporter at one of the lunches
  • Two complimentary conference registrations for the conference
  • Display space during the conference
  • Two company-provided items in conference bag

Silver Sponsor

  • Recognized as Silver Supporter at one of the coffee breaks
  • One company-provided items in conference bag

In-kind Sponsor

We offer the opportunity to sponsor specific events and activities, e.g. student grants, coffee breaks, lunch, reception, or conference banquet. In these cases the sponsor name is prominently displayed during the event and an explicit "thank you" is mentioned at the welcome note of the event.

Contacts

Potential sponsors can send an e-mail to the Conference Finance Chairs, Wei Wang (weiwa@cse.ust.hk) and Yi Wang (wangyi18@huawei.com), with any questions.
